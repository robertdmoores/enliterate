The Carnival From Hell

NOV 2020 | Written by Robert D. Moores with AI Dungeon | Featuring photography by Unsplash with contributions from Robert D. Moores

Summary

  • Murderous clowns, depraved carnies, disgusting food.
  • Violence, gore, acts of atrocity.
  • Sacrilege and blasphemy.
  • Painstakingly crafted to shock and offend.
  • Contains sexual violence, abusive violence, suicide, and worse.

The Setup...

From afar, the carnival is exactly what you'd expect. Colorful tents and flashing lights draw your eye from every direction at once. Throngs of people mill about, throwing balls, waving glowsticks, and chugging large sodas like it's their last day on Earth. The midway is packed with every kind of mechanical contraption from roller-coasters to space wheels. Off in the distance you see an enormous Ferris wheel towering over it all.

Shouts of mad glee fill the air. Barkers shout their wares and prices, promising endless fun to everyone who passes by. Midway rides grind and whir, while the children around them screech in excitement. In the distance, you can hear a calliope playing a melancholy but strangely whimsical tune you've never heard before.

The smell of carnival food drifts through the warm summer air, making your mouth water. It's sweet, cloying, and starchy. The odor of grilled meat, french fries, cotton candy, and root beer hang as thick in the air as smog.

It's only as you step closer to the gates that things start to seem... off. The clowns look half-mad. The sounds of the children seem as much like cries of agony as joy. The smell of food has a murky, rotten-egg fragrance simmering underneath it.

As you stand at the gates and look inside, you swear you see a live lion just... wandering through the crowd.

And clowns! Wow, there are a lot of clowns!
